About Me

Hey there, thanks for checking out my portolio page! I was born on November 16th, 1984 in Oshkosh Wisconsin, and grew up in Appleton Wisconsin. In my teenage years, I loved writing, playing video games, watching anime, making amateur anime video game websites over AOL dial-up, and I dreamed of becoming a video-game designer.

I never went to college, but I always wanted to be an entrepeneur. I really took a liking to Artificial Intelligence after reading about the Singularity. I dabbled a bit in building my own Texas Hold'em Poker AI, but it was never able to win me any money in real online games.

In 2020 I started the Full Stack Flex program through Denver University, in an effort to learn more about web development and become a better product manager. And here I am!
